Site Plan: Explore Lagos

Site Name

Name: Explore Lagos

Reason: This name is simple, action-oriented, and immediately tells the visitor the purpose of the website. It is easy to remember for tourists planning a trip.

Optional Domain: explore-lagos-guide.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a digital weekend guide for visitors to Lagos, Nigeria. It provides curated recommendations for top attractions, authentic local dining, and practical transportation tips to help tourists navigate the city safely and enjoyably. It will also feature an interactive packing list built with JavaScript.

Scenarios

These are questions our target audience (tourists and weekend visitors) will be asking when they arrive on the site:

  • What are the best places to eat authentic Nigerian food near Victoria Island?
  • How do I safely navigate transportation from the airport to my hotel?
  • What essential items should I pack for a weekend trip during the rainy season?

Color Schema

The colors are inspired by the Nigerian flag (Green) and the vibrant, sunny energy of the city (Gold). Notice that this site plan document is styled using these exact colors.

Primary
#008751
Secondary
#F2C94C
Background
#F9F9F9
Text
#333333
  • Primary (Deep Green): Used for the header background, major headings, and primary buttons.
  • Secondary (Golden Yellow): Used for accents, section dividers, and hover effects on links.
  • Background (Off-White): Used for the main body background to keep the site looking clean without the harshness of pure white.
  • Text (Dark Charcoal): Used for all paragraph text to ensure high contrast and readability.

Typography

We are using two Google Fonts for a clean, modern look. These fonts are actively applied to this document.

  • Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif. Applied to h1, h2, and h3 elements for a bold, structural look.
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif. Applied to all paragraphs and lists for excellent readability on small screens.
