Essential Travel Tips

🏧

Currency

The Nigerian Naira (₦) is the local currency. Carry cash for markets and street food. ATMs are widely available on Victoria Island and in Lekki.

🚕

Getting Around

Use Bolt or Uber for safe, metered rides. Avoid using unregistered taxis. Lagos traffic (known as "go-slow") is worst on weekday mornings and evenings.

🌡️

Weather

Lagos is tropical year-round, averaging 27°C. The rainy season runs from May to October. Always pack a light rain jacket and stay hydrated.

🔒

Safety

Lagos is generally safe for tourists in areas like Lekki, VI, and Ikoyi. Keep your belongings secure, avoid late-night walks, and trust your instincts.
